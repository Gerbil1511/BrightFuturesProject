What is Diversity?

Diversity is when there are many different kinds of people, things, or ideas all together. Imagine you have a big box of crayons with lots of different colors like red, blue, green, and yellow. Each color is unique and special in its own way, and when you use all of them together, you can create a beautiful and interesting picture.In the same way, people are different too.

Some have different skin colors, some speak different languages and some have different customs or traditions. This diversity makes the world a more interesting and fun place to live in, just like how all the different crayon colors make your picture special. So, diversity is all about celebrating and appreciating all the unique things that make each person special.

What is Inclusion?

Inclusion is when everyone feels welcome and included, no matter how different they are. Imagine you're playing a game with your friends, and you make sure that every single person gets to join in and have fun. You don't leave anyone out, and you make sure everyone feels happy and part of the group.

In the same way, inclusion means making sure that everyone, no matter what they look like, where they come from, or what they can do, feels like they belong. It's about being kind, respectful, and making sure everyone has an equal chance to participate and enjoy things together. So, inclusion is all about creating a friendly and fair environment where everyone feels valued and included.
